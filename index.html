Who Is
Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive who serves as the CEO of Google and its parent company, Alphabet. He was born on June 10, 1972, in Chennai, India. Pichai studied metallurgical engineering at IIT Kharagpur, completed his master’s degree at Stanford University, and later earned an MBA from the Wharton School. He joined Google in 2004, where he worked on important products like Google Toolbar, Google Chrome, Chrome OS, and Android. His successful leadership in these major projects led to his promotion as CEO of Google in 2015, and later CEO of Alphabet in 2019. Today, Sundar Pichai is recognized as one of the most influential leaders in global technology, known for his humble personality, strong vision, and contribution to innovations used by billions worldwide.
